Man on The Moon - Kid Cudi

For not a few artists this album is the reason they write music the way they do. Kid Cudi has influenced a whole generation of rappers and has saved many people by rapping and singing about his own struggles in life.
Man on the Moon is the first part of a trilogy, which perfectly blends Hip-Hop with Alternative Rock.
