in Rainbows - Radiohead

In Rainbows was released in 2007 and is the seventh studio album of Radiohead. No song sounds like a previous song, not even like an other song on the album, which is quite unique in the music industry, but certainly not for Radiohead.
In 2019 the band was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
